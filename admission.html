Our Simple 4-Step Admissions Process

1

Inquiry & Visit

Begin your journey by learning about our school and experiencing our community firsthand.

  • Request Information
  • Schedule a Campus Tour
  • Meet Faculty & Staff
  • Observe Classes
2

Application Submission

Complete and submit your application with all required supporting documents.

  • Online Application Form
  • Previous School Records
  • Birth Certificate Copy
  • Passport Photographs
3

Assessment & Interview

Get to know us better while we learn about your child's unique strengths and needs.

  • Student Assessment Test
  • Parent/Guardian Interview
  • Student Interaction Session
  • Academic Records Review
4

Decision & Enrollment

Receive your acceptance and complete the enrollment process to secure your place.

  • Acceptance Notification
  • Tuition Deposit Payment
  • Enrollment Paperwork
  • Welcome Orientation
