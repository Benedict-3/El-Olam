Our History

Founded in 2010, El-Olam Global Academy began as a small elementary school with a vision to provide quality, values-based education to the children of Port Harcourt and beyond. What started with just 50 students has grown into a comprehensive K-12 institution serving over 800 students across three campuses.

Our journey has been marked by steady growth and unwavering commitment to educational excellence. In 2015, we expanded to include middle school grades, and by 2018, we opened our high school division, completing our vision of providing seamless K-12 education under one unified philosophy.

Throughout our history, we have maintained our core belief in holistic education‚Äînurturing not just academic achievement, but also character development, creativity, critical thinking, and social responsibility. Our student-centered approach ensures that each child receives personalized attention and support to reach their full potential.

Today, El-Olam Global Academy stands as a testament to what dedication, innovation, and community partnership can achieve in education. Our graduates have gone on to excel in universities and careers worldwide, carrying with them the values and knowledge they gained here.
